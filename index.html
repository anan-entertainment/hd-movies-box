Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about downloading and installing CinemaFlix

Is it safe to install the APK from GitHub?

Yes, our APK files are signed with our official developer certificate and hosted on our verified GitHub repository at anan-entertainment/hd-movies-box. Each release is scanned for malware before publishing. The Google Play Protect warning appears because the app is not downloaded from the Google Play Store, but the app itself is completely safe.

Why do I need TestFlight for iOS instead of the App Store?

TestFlight allows us to distribute pre-release versions of our app to testers before it's available on the App Store. This helps us gather feedback and improve the app before the official release. The TestFlight version has all the same features as the upcoming App Store version.

Will enabling "Install Unknown Apps" make my device vulnerable?

Enabling this setting only allows installation from the specific source you select (like Chrome or Firefox). We recommend turning this setting OFF again after installing our app for maximum security. This won't affect CinemaFlix after it's installed.

How do I update the app when a new version is available?

For iOS TestFlight users, you'll receive a notification when an update is available through TestFlight. For Android users, we'll notify you through the app when updates are available, or you can check the GitHub releases page for the latest version.

I'm having trouble installing the app. What should I do?

Please contact our support team at ananentertainment1601@gmail.com with details about your device model, operating system version, and the specific issue you're experiencing. We'll help you troubleshoot the installation process.
